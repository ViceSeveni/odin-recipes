Singapore Sling

Ingredients:
  • 1 highball glass
  • Ice cubes
  • 1 1/2 Oz Gin
  • 2 Oz Orange Soda
  • 1 Oz Lime Juice
  • 1/2 Oz Grenadine
  • 1 Oz Club Soda
  • 1 Orange Flag

Directions:
  1. Fill a highball glass with ice cubes.
  2. Add the gin, orange soda, lime juice, and grenadine to the glass.
  3. Add the club soda.
  4. Garnish with an orange flag.
  5. Stir, then enjoy!
